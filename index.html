Information Systems Department

The Information Systems Department (DSI) ITS is located on Jl. Raya ITS, Keputih, Kec. Sukolilo, SBY City, East Java 6011. It has been established since 2001 and be the first Information Systems Study Program at State Universities in Indonesia, in addition to that the ITS Information System curriculum has been used as a reference and Qibla Information System Study Program at other institutions. Currently the Information Systems Department manages two Study Programs: Sarjana (S1) Masters (S2) In 2017 the Information System Department was visited and received ACCREDITATION from the ASEAN University Networking-Quality Assessment (AUN-QA) International Accreditation Board.
